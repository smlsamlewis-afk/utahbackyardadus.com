Local Requirements

What Park City Requires for an ADU

Every Utah city sets its own rules for accessory dwelling units on top of state law. Here's what matters most for Park City properties.

📏

Size & Bedrooms

Accessory apartments in Park City can be up to 1,000 sq ft (or one-third of your primary home's size, whichever is smaller), with a 280 sq ft minimum and a 2-bedroom cap.

🚗

Parking by Bedroom

Park City requires one parking space per bedroom in your accessory apartment, on top of what your main home already requires.

📐

Lot Size

An internal ADU needs a lot of at least 6,000 sq ft. Utah's new SB284 (2026) is also opening the door to detached ADUs on larger qualifying lots.

Ordinances change and every lot is different — we confirm your exact zoning, setbacks, and options during your free site visit, at no cost.
